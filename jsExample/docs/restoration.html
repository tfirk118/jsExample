Besides providing beautiful artwork at reasonable prices, The Art Guild, courtesy of Alaria Johnson, can restore your artwork to its original splendor both traditional and digital means.

The Art Guild can restore paintings from common mishaps such as:

  • Scratches
  • Rips
  • Fading
  • Burns
  • Stains
  • And much, much more!

Obviously, the cost of such works will vary with the amount of damage to repair. Because of which, we ask that you send an email to Art_Guild_Restoration@ArtGuild.com with an attached photo of the artwork to be repaired; the higher the quality the better! Within 5 to 7 business days we will email you back with a quote on how much the restoration will cost and instructions on how to get your artwork to us.


Thank you for considering The Art Guild for your artwork restoration needs!
